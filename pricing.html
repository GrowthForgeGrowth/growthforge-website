Frequently Asked Questions About GrowthForge Pricing

What's the difference between a Growth Audit and a Discovery Session?

A Growth Discovery Session is a complimentary 20-minute qualification call to determine if GrowthForge is the right partner. A Growth Audit ($750) is a comprehensive strategic assessment with detailed deliverables including business analysis, market analysis, technical SEO audit, AI readiness assessment, competitor analysis, and a 90-day growth roadmap with executive presentation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my partnership tier?

Yes. Most businesses start with Growth Partner and scale to Growth Accelerator or Growth Engine as they see results and require additional capabilities. You can adjust your partnership level based on your evolving needs and budget.

What's included in support for each tier?

Growth Partner includes email support and monthly strategy sessions. Growth Accelerator includes bi-weekly strategy calls and priority support. Growth Engine includes weekly leadership meetings and priority implementation planning. All partnerships have a dedicated Growth Partner (senior strategist) assigned to your account.

What are the contract terms?

Growth partnerships require a minimum 3-month commitment. We recommend 12+ month partnerships to see meaningful, compounding growth results and sustainable business impact.

Can I add Growth Accelerators to my partnership?

Yes. Growth Accelerators (Landing Page Design, Technical SEO Audit, AI Workflow Automation, CRM Automation, AI Chatbot, Executive Growth Workshop, Programmatic SEO, Custom Analytics Dashboard) can be added to any partnership tier to enhance and accelerate results.

What if I'm not ready to commit long-term?

Start with a Growth Audit ($750). This gives you a strategic assessment, competitive analysis, AI readiness review, and a clear 90-day roadmap. You can then explore a Growth Partner engagement with full confidence and clarity.

How do you measure success and growth?

Every partnership includes a KPI Dashboard with transparent metrics aligned to your business goals. We track what matters: qualified leads, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, lifetime value, revenue impact, and other metrics specific to your business.

What happens if I'm not seeing results?

We're transparent about what's working and what's not. If there's not a good fit or clear path to results, we'll tell you honestly. Most partnerships see meaningful progress by month 2-3, with compound results increasing over time.
