Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a Growth Audit and a Discovery Session?

A Discovery Session ($0, 20 minutes) is a qualification call to see if GrowthForge is the right partner. A Growth Audit ($750) is a comprehensive strategic assessment with detailed deliverables and a 90-day roadmap—perfect for businesses ready to explore a deeper engagement.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my partnership level?

Yes. Most businesses start with Growth Partner and scale to Growth Accelerator or Growth Engine as they see results. You can adjust your partnership level based on your needs and budget.

What's included in support?

Growth Partner includes email support. Growth Accelerator includes bi-weekly calls and priority support. Growth Engine includes weekly meetings and priority implementation planning. All partnerships have a dedicated Growth Partner assigned to your account.

Do you offer contracts?

Yes. Growth partnerships require a minimum 3-month commitment. We recommend longer commitments to see meaningful results and compound growth. Most of our best partnerships span 12+ months.

Can I add Growth Accelerators to any partnership?

Yes. Growth Accelerators (like AI Automation, Custom Dashboards, Programmatic SEO) can be added to any partnership tier to enhance and accelerate results.

What if I'm not ready to commit long-term?

Start with a Growth Audit ($750). This gives you a strategic roadmap, competitive analysis, and clear next steps. You can then explore a Growth Partner engagement with full confidence.
