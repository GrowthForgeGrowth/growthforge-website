Trust & Content Policy

GrowthForge Editorial Standards

This page explains how GrowthForge researches, writes, reviews, updates, and corrects content across our AI SEO, GEO, paid media, CRO, analytics, and strategy pages.

Editorial oversight by Charlie Chan, Founder & Growth Strategist at GrowthForge. Commercial, educational, and reference pages are written to support buyer clarity first and search visibility second.

Why these standards exist

GrowthForge publishes content for operators and executives making real budget, channel, and growth decisions. Our editorial process is designed to keep claims aligned with visible evidence, preserve commercial clarity, and make important pages easy for both buyers and AI systems to interpret accurately.

Accuracy over volume

We prefer fewer pages with clearer reasoning, tighter definitions, and stronger internal links over large volumes of generic content.

Commercial clarity

Service, pricing, and contact pages should make the business model legible before a discovery call.

Visible trust signals

Authorship, methodology, pricing, and contact paths should be visible on-page rather than implied or hidden in markup.

How GrowthForge creates and reviews content

Authorship

Core strategy, service, trust, and reference pages are drafted or approved by Charlie Chan. Commercial pages are expected to reflect GrowthForge's actual service model and operating method.

Subject review

Pages covering AI SEO, GEO, analytics, CRO, and growth strategy are reviewed for internal consistency with our published positioning, offers, and contact information.

Scannable structure

We use clear headings, answer-first paragraphs, lists, and concise takeaways so readers can evaluate ideas quickly and AI systems can extract context more reliably.

Sourcing, updates, and corrections

Factual claims

We avoid publishing client claims, certifications, statistics, or partner references unless they can be supported visibly on the page or through public documentation.

Periodic review

Trust pages, service pages, and pricing content are updated when the operating model, offers, or public guidance changes. Supporting insight pages are reviewed when terminology or search behavior materially changes.

Corrections

If you find an accuracy issue, email charlie@growthforge-ai.com with the page URL, the statement in question, and your suggested correction or source.

How we use AI in content production

GrowthForge uses AI as a drafting, analysis, and structuring aid — not as a substitute for editorial judgment.

  • AI may assist with outlining, summarizing, or reorganizing material for clarity.
  • Human review is required before publishing commercial, trust, or reference content.
  • We do not publish synthetic testimonials, fabricated partner mentions, or unverifiable performance claims.
  • Structured data should reflect visible content on the page exactly.

Commercial disclosure and page intent

GrowthForge publishes both educational and commercial content. Pages such as Insights and the AI Search Authority Checklist are designed to help buyers think more clearly about modern search visibility. Service and pricing pages exist to explain how GrowthForge can help execute that work. We aim to make the transition between education and commercial evaluation explicit, not hidden.
