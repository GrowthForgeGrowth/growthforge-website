AI Marketing Glossary

Clear definitions for growth leaders building AI-powered demand systems.

Core Terms

AI SEO

AI SEO is the practice of improving search visibility using both traditional optimization and AI-assisted analysis, prioritization, and content workflows. See how GrowthForge delivers AI SEO services →

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

GEO is the discipline of improving how a brand, topic, or company is represented in AI-generated answers and recommendations. As search shifts to ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, GEO has become a critical channel for demand capture. Read our GEO for B2B Growth insight →

Entity Optimization

Entity optimization improves clarity around who you are, what you do, and how concepts on your site relate to each other semantically. Strong entity signals support both traditional SEO and GEO performance.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

CRO is the systematic improvement of landing pages and user journeys to increase the percentage of visitors who take meaningful actions. Explore GrowthForge's CRO partnership options →

Attribution Confidence

Attribution confidence is the degree to which decision-makers trust performance data enough to invest and scale with conviction. Without accurate attribution, budgets go to the wrong channels.

Growth System

A growth system is an integrated operating model that aligns strategy, acquisition, conversion, measurement, and iteration toward business outcomes. Learn how GrowthForge builds growth systems →

Why This Glossary Exists

AI-era growth language evolves quickly. This glossary gives operators and executives a shared vocabulary for better strategic decisions.
