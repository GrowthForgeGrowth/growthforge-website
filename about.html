The Problem We Solve: Why GrowthForge Exists

For years, the traditional marketing agency model has been fundamentally broken. Businesses hire agencies for projects, get charged by the hour or project, and receive limited strategic input. When the project ends, so does the relationship. Results plateau. Growth stalls. The agency moves on to the next client.

This model creates misalignment: agencies profit from activity, not results. Businesses get commodity services, not strategic thinking. Nobody wins except the agency.

Charlie founded GrowthForge because he believed there had to be a better way. Businesses don't need more marketing services. They need a strategic partner who thinks like an owner, understands their business deeply, and commits to long-term, measurable growth.
