Frequently Asked Questions About GrowthForge

What is a growth consultancy and how is it different from a marketing agency?

A growth consultancy like GrowthForge takes a strategic partnership approach focused on building predictable growth systems. Unlike traditional marketing agencies that charge for isolated services, we become an extension of your team, aligned with your business metrics, and committed to your long-term success. We think like business partners, not vendors.

How does GrowthForge use AI in growth strategy?

We use AI across multiple dimensions: AI SEO for search visibility optimization, AI-powered Google Ads and Meta Ads bidding and targeting, marketing automation to scale execution, predictive analytics for customer insights, and AI chatbots for lead qualification. AI allows us to execute at scale without proportional cost increases.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and why does it matter?

Generative Engine Optimization is optimizing your content and website for AI search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity. As AI transforms how people search and discover information, GEO ensures your business is visible in these emerging search channels. GrowthForge specializes in both traditional SEO and GEO.

How do I know if GrowthForge is the right partner for my business?

Schedule a complimentary 20-minute Growth Discovery Session. This is a mutual qualification meeting to understand your business and determine if there's a good fit. We're selective about partnerships to ensure every client receives the attention and strategic focus they deserve.

What should I expect from a Growth Audit?

A Growth Audit ($750) is a comprehensive strategic assessment that includes business analysis, market analysis, technical SEO audit, AI readiness assessment, paid media review, conversion opportunity analysis, competitor analysis, and a 90-day growth roadmap with executive presentation.

What's the minimum commitment for a growth partnership?

Growth partnerships (Growth Partner, Growth Accelerator, Growth Engine) require a minimum 3-month commitment. We recommend 12+ month partnerships to see meaningful, compounding growth results.
