Frequently Asked Questions About GrowthForge

What is a growth consultancy and how is it different from a marketing agency?

A growth consultancy like GrowthForge takes a strategic partnership approach focused on building predictable growth systems. Unlike traditional marketing agencies that charge for isolated services, we combine strategy, execution, optimization, and AI-enabled systems to drive long-term business outcomes.

How does GrowthForge use AI in growth strategy?

We use AI across multiple dimensions: AI SEO for search visibility optimization, AI-powered Google Ads and Meta Ads bidding and targeting, marketing automation to scale execution, predictive analytics, and workflow automation for faster and better performance.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and why does it matter?

Generative Engine Optimization is optimizing your content and website for AI search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity. As AI transforms how people search, GEO helps maintain visibility and authority in AI-generated results.

How do I know if GrowthForge is the right partner for my business?

Schedule a complimentary 20-minute Growth Discovery Session. This is a mutual qualification meeting to understand your business and determine if there's a good fit. We're selective about partnerships because we only work where we can create measurable impact.

What should I expect from a Growth Audit?

A Growth Audit ($750) is a comprehensive strategic assessment that includes business analysis, market analysis, technical SEO audit, AI readiness assessment, paid media review, conversion opportunities, and a 90-day implementation roadmap.

What's the minimum commitment for a growth partnership?

Growth partnerships (Growth Partner, Growth Accelerator, Growth Engine) require a minimum 3-month commitment. We recommend 12+ month partnerships to see meaningful, compounding growth results.
