What Is AI SEO?

AI SEO is the practice of improving search visibility using both traditional optimization and AI-assisted analysis, content strategy, and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). Unlike traditional SEO, which targets ranked positions in Google's organic results, AI SEO incorporates strategies for appearing in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity.

AI SEO encompasses technical site health, keyword and topic strategy, structured content for AI extractability, and entity optimization — all integrated toward measurable pipeline outcomes. See how GrowthForge applies AI SEO for B2B SaaS companies →

What Is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the discipline of optimizing content and entity signals so that AI-powered search engines cite your brand in their generated answers. As search shifts from blue-link results to AI-synthesized responses, GEO has become a required component of any complete search strategy.

How GEO differs from traditional SEO:

  • Traditional SEO optimizes for keyword rankings and click-through rates in Google's organic results.
  • GEO optimizes for citation inclusion in AI-generated answers — where there are no ranked positions, only sources cited or ignored.

GEO requires structured, authoritative, and scannable content that AI models can extract and attribute. Learn more about GrowthForge's GEO services → or explore all AI marketing definitions →
