What is a Discovery Session?

This is NOT a free consulting call. This is NOT a free strategy session. This is NOT a free audit.

A Growth Discovery Session is a mutual qualification meeting designed to:

  • Help us understand your business, goals, and growth challenges
  • Assess whether GrowthForge is the right strategic partner
  • Determine if there's a good fit and aligned expectations
  • Discuss next steps if we decide to move forward

If you're looking for strategic recommendations or a detailed audit, we recommend starting with a Growth Audit ($750) instead. This gives you a comprehensive assessment with actionable insights and a 90-day roadmap.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a sales call?

It's a mutual qualification meeting. We want to understand your business, and you want to understand how we work. If there's not a good fit, we'll tell you honestly.

Can I get strategic recommendations during the session?

Not really—20 minutes isn't enough for a thorough strategic assessment. If you want detailed recommendations, we offer a Growth Audit ($750) which includes a comprehensive assessment, competitor analysis, and a 90-day roadmap.

What if I'm interested but not ready to commit yet?

Perfect. We can discuss timeline and help you understand what a partnership would look like. Growth Audits are also a great first step if you want strategic clarity before committing to a long-term partnership.

How do you schedule calls?
