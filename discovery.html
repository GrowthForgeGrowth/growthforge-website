What is a Growth Discovery Session?

This is NOT a free consulting call. This is NOT a free strategy session. This is NOT a free audit.

A Growth Discovery Session is a mutual qualification meeting designed to:

  • Help us understand your business, goals, and growth challenges
  • Assess whether GrowthForge is the right strategic partner for your situation
  • Determine if there's a good fit and aligned expectations
  • Discuss partnership options and next steps if we decide to move forward together

If you're looking for strategic recommendations or a detailed growth audit, we recommend starting with a Growth Audit ($750) instead. This comprehensive engagement includes business analysis, market analysis, technical SEO audit, AI readiness assessment, competitor analysis, and a detailed 90-day growth roadmap with executive presentation.

Frequently Asked Questions About Discovery Sessions

Is this a sales call or free consulting session?

It's a mutual qualification meeting. We want to understand your business, and you want to understand how we work and whether we're the right fit. If there's not a good fit, we'll tell you honestly. It's not a free strategy session or free audit—those are separate paid services.

Can I get strategic recommendations during the session?

Not really—20 minutes isn't enough for thorough strategic assessment. If you want detailed recommendations with analysis, we offer a Growth Audit ($750) which includes comprehensive assessment, competitor analysis, AI readiness review, and a 90-day growth roadmap with executive presentation.

What if I'm interested but not ready to commit yet?

Perfect. We can discuss timeline and help you understand what a partnership would look like. Growth Audits are also a great first step if you want strategic clarity before committing to a long-term partnership. Or schedule a discovery session with a future start date in mind.

How do you schedule calls?

Once you submit the form, we'll respond within 24 hours with available time slots. You'll book directly on our calendar. We typically schedule calls Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm PT.

What if I prefer a different time zone or day?

Let us know in the form and we'll do our best to accommodate. Email charlie@growthforge-ai.com if you have specific scheduling constraints we should know about.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's okay. Just fill out the form with whatever information you can. We'll explore your situation during the discovery session and help you understand what makes sense for your business and budget.

What happens if there's a good fit?

We'll discuss which partnership tier (Growth Partner, Growth Accelerator, Growth Engine) makes sense for your situation, pricing, contract terms, next steps, and timeline. We'll answer all your questions before moving forward.

What if there's not a good fit?

We'll be honest about it. It's better to know early if the fit isn't right. We'd rather be transparent than commit to something that won't work for your business.

Can't Find a Time That Works?

Email us at charlie@growthforge-ai.com and we'll do our best to find a time that works for your schedule.

We're flexible and committed to making this work if there's mutual interest.
