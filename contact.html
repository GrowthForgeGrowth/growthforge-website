Frequently Asked Questions About Contacting GrowthForge

What's the best way to reach GrowthForge?

Email charlie@growthforge-ai.com for general inquiries. If you want to discuss a potential growth partnership, schedule a complimentary Growth Discovery Session at growthforge-ai.com/discovery. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all emails within 24 hours during business hours (Mon-Fri). If you submit a discovery session request, we'll send available time slots within 24 hours.

Can I ask questions before scheduling a discovery session?

Absolutely. Email charlie@growthforge-ai.com with any questions about our services, partnerships, pricing, or approach. We're happy to discuss before you commit to a discovery session.

What should I include in my inquiry email?

Include your company name, industry, main growth challenge or question, and what prompted you to reach out. This helps us provide a more relevant response. But even a simple 'I have questions' is fine—we'll take it from there.

Can I book a discovery session directly from this page?

Yes. Use the contact form below to send a message. Or go directly to growthforge-ai.com/discovery to schedule a session. Both routes get to the same place—we'll respond within 24 hours with available times.

What if I want to discuss pricing before a discovery session?

That's great. Email charlie@growthforge-ai.com and include your company, industry, main growth goal, and approximate monthly marketing budget. We can discuss partnership options and pricing before a formal discovery session.

Are there other ways to contact GrowthForge?

Primary contact is email: charlie@growthforge-ai.com. You can also book a discovery session at growthforge-ai.com/discovery or use the contact form on this page. We prefer email for initial inquiries as it lets us respond thoughtfully.

What if my question is urgent?

Mark your email 'URGENT' in the subject line and we'll prioritize it. For existing clients, reach out through your dedicated channel. We aim to respond to urgent emails within a few hours during business hours.
